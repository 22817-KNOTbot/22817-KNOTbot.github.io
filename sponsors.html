Sponsors

We have a mission to learn and develop the many crucial skills robotics teaches us. However, participating in robotics is not cheap. That is why our team would not be possible without the generous support of sponsors. We appreciate donations of any amount.

Sponsorship Details

Robotics is a rapidly growing industry that will one day shape our future. It develops people in many different aspects of life, helping them grow into better versions of themselves. A big setback for many FTC teams, however, is the expense of running and maintaining a high-performance robot. This is where sponsorships play a key role in helping teams succeed. At Team 22817 Knotbot, we showcase our sponsors by placing their logos on our robot, displaying them on banners at competitions, and wearing them on team hoodies at events. We also highlight and talk about our sponsors during outreach presentations in schools and community programs, ensuring their support is recognized while inspiring others to explore the world of robotics.
