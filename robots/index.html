Our Robot

FTC Centerstage 2023-2024

As our rookie year, we focused on developing our robotics skills and learning how FTC functioned. Our robot features a mecanum drivetrain, an active intake, and a pulley-based hang system. This robot served as a foundation for the improvements made in future competitions.

FTC: Into The Deep 2024-2025

In our 2nd year of FTC, this robot is the culmination of our 1st year of learning and over six months of hard work. Full of innovative uses of 3D printing and featuring over ten degrees of freedom, this is our best robot yet. Click here to learn more.

Cross-District Challenge

We were lucky to be invited to participate in a cross-district fire fighting competition. The object of this competition is to put out as many candle fires as possible while trying to avoid obstacles, picking up balls, and getting the best time. Click here to learn more.
