Funding

Management of Finances

The management of our finances is one of our highest priorities. We have chosen a member dedicated to treasury to fully monitor the important details of our funds. Among many other jobs, our treasurer creates spreadsheets to monitor and observe any changes to our balance. Any and all expenses are logged to ensure we have full control and knowledge about our finances at any given time. This allows us to budget and plan ahead accordingly, allowing us to use our money effectively.

Fundraising

In the past we have done occasional fundraising and grant applications, which became our main source of income. This year, we plan to constantly hold fundraisers as we need to raise money for two teams: the KNOTbot team and the junior team. We have an entire team of volunteers solely reserved for fundraising. We have planned a cookie fundraiser which is scheduled for the near future, and many more to come. All our earned money goes to a good cause, allowing us to pursue great things.

Use of Funds

Most of our funds are used for robot parts such as linear slides, odometry pods, and more. Some products can cost up to a few hundred dollars per piece, making fundraising crucial. Supporting Us

Participating in robotics is not cheap, yet we still believe it is valuable. That is why we continue to participate in the FTC competition, despite the many challenges. Reaching our fullest potential is not possible without support and generosity. If you wish to support our mission, the best way is to sponsor us.
