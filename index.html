Our Mission

We believe our team should be more than building robots. By having an end goal, we learn to work towards great things, one step at a time. Robotics encourages people to step out of their comfort zone and try something new. It stimulates collaboration with people we normally would not and allows the use technology which is normally inaccessible. Due to the vast nature of robotics, it allows us to learn and teach the many involved subjects: math, physics, engineering, technology use, software development, and much more. Not only does it provoke intellectual growth, we practice goal setting, time management, problem solving, and critical thinking. We believe participating in robotics encourages us to become better people.

Our Work

We primarily participate in the FirstÂ® Tech Challenge. Historically we have received the Innovate Award and placed high in tournaments. The current season is INTO THE DEEPSM presented by RTX. More details can be found by watching this video. In summary, there are 2 main match periods. In the first the robot must autonomously perform tasks, and in the second a driver may control it. The robot must pick up scoring elements which can be scored in one of two main ways. It may deposit a scoring element into a basket or attach a clip and hang it.

We have worked hard on our robot for multiple weeks. It contains numerous mechanisms such as a holonomic drive train, linear slides, and a dropdown arm. We have many more ambitious goals we wish to accomplish this season. More details can be found on Our Robot.
